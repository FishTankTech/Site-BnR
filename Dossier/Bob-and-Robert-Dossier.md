## The Cataloging Process

Bob && Robert combines hands-on archival fieldwork with modern documentation practices:

- **On-site cataloging:** Direct, in-person inventory and condition assessment of the physical collection.
- **High-resolution photography:** Professional documentation of each piece, shot and processed using a Canon-based imaging workflow.
- **Digital archive & virtual museum:** A structured, searchable digital record of the collection, built to support research, insurance, exhibition planning, and public access — independent of physical location.
- **Provenance research:** Ongoing work to establish and document the history of each piece.

This process is designed to make the collection "museum-ready" — every piece arrives with full documentation, condition notes, and provenance research already in hand.
