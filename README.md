# Bob & Robert — The Virtual Estate

This repository hosts the site for **Bob & Robert: The Virtual Estate**, a digital catalog presenting works by **Robert Cremean** ("Bob") and **Robert de Lavergne**.

The site is a portal into the studio and estate collection, organized into a few core sections:

- **Available** — curator's picks and pieces currently available for acquisition
- **Collection** — a browsable survey of the full body of work (in development)
- **Materials** — supplemental content including sketchbooks, writings, and press
- **Contact** — acquisitions and general inquiries
- **About** — background pages for both Robert de Lavergne and "Bob" Cremean

The project catalogs and archives a fine art estate collection, with content curated from the Cremean and de Lavergne estate.
